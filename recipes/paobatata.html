Pão de batata recheado

Description:

Healthy potate bread filled with chicken and muzzarela cheese.

  • 35 gr Oat flour
  • 250gr Potato
  • 50gr Shredded chicken
  • 30gr Muzzarela cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasoning to taste

How To Cook It

  1. Cook the potate and then mix with the oat flour, until obtain an uniform mass
  2. Separete the mass into 4 smaller balls
  3. Open each ball like a purse and then put the shredded chicken and muzzarela inside
  4. Close and make the form of a small ball again
  5. Put some egg or olive oli on the top so is more golden after baking
  6. Take the balls to the Air frier or hoven for 30 minutes on 180C degrees
