Banana Bread Fit

Description:

Healthy low sugar but delicious banana bread :)

  • 100 gr Oat flour
  • 50gr Espelta Flour
  • 50gr Whole Flour
  • 4 Eggs
  • 30gr Sugar
  • 30gr Vanilla Veggan Powder
  • Cinemon
  • 35gr non salty Butter
  • 50gr of Nuts
  • 20gr Baking Powder

How To Cook It

  1. Pre-heat the hoven at 180C
  2. Mix well the eggs and sugar
  3. Add the flours and protein powder
  4. Smash the bananas with the butter and the cinemon and add to the mixture
  5. Mix everything well, until a uniform mass
  6. Add the baking powder and mix a bit more
  7. Put in the mold and add the nuts on top
  8. Take to the hoven for 40 minutes
