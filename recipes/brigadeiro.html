Brigadeiro de colher

Description:

Brigadeiro de colher, typical brazilian dessert.

  • Condensed milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles
  • Unsalted butter
  • A pinch of salt

How To Cook It

  1. Mix the condensed milk, the butter, and the cocoa powder in a medium non-stick pan.
  2. The mixture thickens after about 10-12 minutes, and once you can run your silicone spatula through the middle of it without it running back together for 2-3 seconds, it is done.
  3. Pour the mixture onto the lightly greased plate and let it chill in the fridge for an hour.
  4. Once chilled, make a 'croqueta' form in a spoon, put some choc sprinkle on top and enjoy!
